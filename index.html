Cash Payment Drivers

02
Above-Average Earnings
Drivers whose total payment exceeds fleet average (subquery)
▾
SELECT D.D_name, SUM(P.amount) AS total_earnings FROM Driver D JOIN Ride R ON D.Driver_id = R.Driver_id JOIN Payment P ON R.Ride_id = P.Ride_id GROUP BY D.Driver_id HAVING SUM(P.amount) > (SELECT AVG(t) FROM (...) sub);

03
Universal Driver Profile — VIEW
Aggregated view: vehicle, avg rating, accident count per driver
▾
CREATE VIEW Universal_Driver_Profile AS SELECT D.D_name, V.model, AVG(F.rating) AS avg_rating, COUNT(A.Report_id) AS accidents FROM Driver D LEFT JOIN Vehicle V ON D.Driver_id = V.Driver_id LEFT JOIN Ride R ON D.Driver_id = R.Driver_id LEFT JOIN Feedback F ON R.Ride_id = F.Ride_id LEFT JOIN Accident_Report A ON D.Driver_id = A.Driver_id GROUP BY D.Driver_id;

04
Customer Ride Count
LEFT JOIN to include customers with zero rides
▾
SELECT C.c_name, COUNT(R.Ride_id) AS ride_count FROM Customer C LEFT JOIN Ride R ON C.Customer_id = R.Customer_id GROUP BY C.Customer_id;

Safe Drivers — No Major Accidents
Drivers excluded from Major accident reports (NOT IN subquery)
▾
SELECT D.D_name, D.license_no FROM Driver D WHERE D.Driver_id NOT IN ( SELECT Driver_id FROM Accident_Report WHERE severity = 'Major' );
